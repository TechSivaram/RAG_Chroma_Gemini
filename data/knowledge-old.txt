The "GreenTech Solutions" company was founded in 2020 with a mission to develop sustainable energy solutions. Their flagship product is the "SolarFlow Home Battery," an innovative energy storage system for residential use. It boasts a capacity of 15 kWh and a lifespan of 10 years. GreenTech is headquartered in San Francisco, California, and plans to expand its operations to Europe by 2025. They are currently developing a new wind turbine technology called "AeroGen."
